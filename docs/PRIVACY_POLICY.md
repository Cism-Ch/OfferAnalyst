# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 22, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to OfferAnalyst ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
- **Profile Information**: Optional profile details you choose to add
- **User Content**: Search queries, analysis configurations, saved offers, and project data
- **API Keys**: Third-party API keys you provide for BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) functionality

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the service
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Cookies**: Session cookies for authentication and preferences

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain our services
- Process your requests and analyze offers using AI models
- Authenticate your account and maintain security
- Improve our services and develop new features
- Communicate with you about service updates
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

- API keys are hashed using bcrypt before storage
- All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
- MongoDB database with encryption at rest
- Regular security audits and updates
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse

### Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Search history: Retained based on your preferences
- Logs: Kept for 90 days for security purposes
- Deleted account data: Permanently removed within 30 days

## Third-Party Services

### AI Model Providers

We use OpenRouter to access various AI models. When you use our service:

- Your search queries and offer data are sent to AI models for analysis
- OpenRouter's privacy policy applies to their data processing
- We do not share your personal account information with AI providers

### OAuth Providers

If you sign in with Google or GitHub:

- We only receive your email and name
- We do not access your other Google/GitHub data
- You can revoke access anytime through your OAuth provider

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Disable certain data collection features

## BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)

When you provide your own API keys:

- Keys are encrypted before storage
- Keys are only used for your requests
- You maintain control and can revoke access anytime
- Usage is subject to your provider's terms

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.
